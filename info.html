1. Platinum resistance thermometer reads 3Ω at 0°C and 8Ω at 100°C. If it reads 6Ω, find the temperature.
θ = [(Rθ − R₀)/(R₁₀₀ − R₀)] × 100 θ = [(6 − 3)/(8 − 3)] × 100 θ = (3/5) × 100 θ = 60°C

4. 250 g of lead at 170°C is dropped into 100 g of water at 0°C. Final temperature = 12°C. Find specific heat capacity.
Heat lost = Heat gained 250 × c × (170 − 12) = 100 × 4200 × (12 − 0) 250c × 158 = 100 × 4200 × 12 39500c = 5,040,000 c = 5,040,000 / 39,500 c ≈ 128 J/kgK
5. A 50 W heater evaporates 0.005 kg of water in 50 s. Find latent heat of vaporization.
Energy = Pt = 50 × 50 = 2500 J L = Energy / mass = 2500 / 0.005 = 5.0 × 10⁵ J/kg
6. A 500 kg car initially at rest accelerates at 5 m/s². Find its kinetic energy after 4 s.
v = u + at = 0 + (5 × 4) = 20 m/s KE = ½mv² = ½ × 500 × (20)² = 100,000 J
7. A man of mass 50 kg runs up a flight of stairs 5 m high in 5 s. Find power.
Work = mgh = 50 × 10 × 5 = 2500 J Power = Work / time = 2500 / 5 = 500 W
8. A car of mass 800 kg reaches 25 m/s in 20 s. Find power developed.
KE = ½mv² = ½ × 800 × (25)² = 250,000 J Power = 250,000 / 20 = 12,500 W
